Who are we?

Blue Crystal Limited, a captivating jewelry company based in
Nairobi, Kenya, was envisioned and brought to life by Brian as part of his school
project in 2023. Brian's creative vision and passion for exquisite craftsmanship
led him to establish a brand that reflects the beauty and diversity of Kenyan culture.
Blue Crystal Limited specializes in crafting stunning jewelry pieces that showcase a
harmonious blend of traditional and contemporary designs, incorporating locally sourced
gemstones and materials.The company not only aims tocreate aesthetically pleasing
accessories but also strives to contribute to the local economy by promoting skilled
craftsmanship. Through Brian's dedication and innovative approach, Blue Crystal Limited
has become a shining example of how entrepreneurship can be a catalyst for both artistic
expression and community development.
